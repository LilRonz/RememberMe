Amour de ma vie.


Dear Jucel (langga),

I hope this message brings a smile to your face as you read it. I want you to know just how

much you truly mean to me. You are not just my girl, but my best friend, my confidant,

and my source of inspiration.



In the time that we've been together, you've taught me the true meaning of love and

compassion. Your kindness and generosity never cease to amaze me. You have a heart of gold,

and I am so lucky to be loved by you.



I know that life can sometimes be challenging, and it's in these moments that I wish I could

do more for you. Please remember that it's okay to feel overwhelmed and it's okay to ask for

help. You don't have to face everything alone. I am here for you, through thick and thin, in

sickness and in health, and for all the highs and lows that life brings our way.



Your strength and resilience inspire me every day. You have faced so many challenges and

yet, you always come out stronger on the other side. I admire your courage and your

determination. And even when things are tough, you still have the ability to find joy in the

little things. That is something truly special.



Please remember to take care of yourself. You are always taking care of others and

sometimes, you forget that you too, need nurturing. Take time to do the things that bring

you happiness. Read a book, listen to your favorite song, dance around the room, take a long

bath. Do whatever it is that makes your soul happy.



And finally, remember that you are loved, not just by me, but by everyone whose lives you

touch. You bring so much joy and happiness into this world and I am grateful every day that

you are in my life.



With all my love and more,

Ronel Olandag
